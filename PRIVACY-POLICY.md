# Privacy Policy for Rebbit

**Last Updated:** April 27, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to Rebbit, a browser extension developed by **duckbox**. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## 2. Information Collection and Use
Rebbit operates entirely locally within your browser. 
- **We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data.**
- All user preferences, settings, and customizations are stored locally on your device using the browser's standard storage API (`chrome.storage.local`).
- We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party cookies.

## 3. Permissions
To function properly, Rebbit requests the following permissions:
- `storage`: Required to save your extension preferences (e.g., theme settings, toggles) locally.
- `activeTab` / `host_permissions`: Required to inject CSS and scripts solely into `reddit.com` to restyle the site. We do not read or record your browsing history.

## 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us via our GitHub repository.
